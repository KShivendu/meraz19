Street Maniacs (Street Battle)

Description:

Dressed for a fight, they prepare to battle as the crowd gathers around. This isn’t a street fight in its true essence, it is a street dance battle where the music begins and the group breaks into dance.

Steps to apply:

  1. The participant has to register here. 
  2. They will have to submit their dance video (duration: 1-2 mins; in mp4 format) performed by them for a preliminary shortlisting.

Deadline:

5th November’2019 

Rules:

The street battle comprises of three rounds.

  1. Showcase Round: In this round, the contestants shall be called up to perform, one by one, on which they will be scored by the judges. The time limit for the performance shall be 40 seconds. ( one should bring their own track for this round).
  2. Face-Off Round: On the basis of the scores of the Showcase Round, you shall be pitched against another contestant in a one on one face-off battle. This Face-Off battle will be of three segments, each segment being a minute long (30 seconds for each contestant in every segment).
  3. Cypher Round : In this round all the winners of the Face-Off Round ( of their respective battles) will gather around in a circle and perform one by one inside the circle ( the time limit for each contestant shall be up to 40 seconds) and the best performers will be declared as the final winners of the street battle.

NOTE: Tracks for the second and final round will be decided by us.

ALSO,

  1. At the time of reporting for the stage round, all are requested to submit the names of the performers along with valid ID proof( viz. College ID card)
  2. Recognized professionals are not allowed to enter the competition.
  3. Reporting is to be done until 30 minutes prior to the start of the competition.
  4. All Dance forms are allowed.
  5. Solo/duet/group all are allowed.
  6. Participants are requested to submit their tracks in .mp3 format well in advance before the start of the event.
  7. Best of eight in the group, among the submitted videos, will be selected and promoted for the showcase round.
  8. After the showcase round, it will be decided that which team are going to compete with each other in the face-off round.
  9. Four teams will be selected from face-off round which will perform in cypher round.
