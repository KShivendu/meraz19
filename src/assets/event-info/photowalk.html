PHOTOWALK

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Photowalk is a photography event of the most awaited techno-cultural fest “MERAZ’19”. Capture your surroundings; pour out your imagination into your photographs along with a caption allowing the photograph to be the best photograph of the event. 

DURATION OF THE EVENT:

90 minutes

LOCATION:

Campus of IIT BHILAI.

THEME OF THE EVENT:

This is an open theme contest.
