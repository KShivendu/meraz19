Depth of Dance ( Solo Dance Competition)

Description:

Do you love to dance?

Start with the man in the mirror.

Start with yourself.

Start with you.

Steps to apply:

  1. The participant has to register here. 
  2. They will have to submit their dance video (duration: 1-2 mins; in mp4 format) performed by them for a preliminary shortlisting.

Deadline:

5th November’2019 

Rules:

  1. At the time of reporting for the onstage round, all are requested to submit all the names of the performers along with valid ID proof (viz. College ID card).
  2. Recognized professionals are not allowed to enter the competition.
  3. Reporting is to be done 30 minutes prior to the start of the competition.
  4. All Dance forms are allowed.
  5. Participants are requested to submit their tracks in .mp3 format well in advance before the start of the event.
  6. After the preliminary round, 4 participants will advance to the stage round.
  7. Two of the best among the stage round performers will be qualified for the final round which is the “Face-Off”.
  8. For the stage round participants are allowed to give a performance of max 3 minutes, any further delay will lead to deduction of the marks.
  9. For the face-off round the song will be given by the judging panel and preparation time will be given.
  10. The participating teams are requested to ensure that their presentation is in keeping with the dignity of the fest. Vulgarity or provocative acts will not be tolerated.
  11. The decision of judges and the organizing team will be final and binding.
  12. For the stage round, participants are requested to stick to the rules, any other cases will lead to deduction of the marks.
