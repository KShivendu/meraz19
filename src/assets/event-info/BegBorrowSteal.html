Beg Borrow steal

 

Rules:

1.A Team will comprise of 2 players

All the teams will be provided with a list of items.

2.Team members will have to complete the task within a time limit of 30 minutes.

3.Team can either beg, borrow or steal the items that are enlisted and provided to them

4.Teams will have to get themselves registered before the commencement of the event.

5.After the completion of the task the teams will are required to report to the coordinators for evaluation.

6.Teams will be judged on first come, first serve basis and also the items they were able to collect.
