Dare to Dare

Rules:

1.)There will be option for individual participation or team participation(team comprises a maximum of 3 members).

2.)Layout-The tasks will be divided in 2 levels. 3 dares will be given in level one.If all the 3 dares are completed successfully, level 2 will come in action, which will be much more tougher than level one. Afterwards, winners will be decided.
